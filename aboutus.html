About Us

We are a one-stop fashion maintenance portal. We aim to connect users with fashion service providers such as tailors, shoes smiths and leather artisans. With fast fashion becoming an increased prevalence as more consumers purchase more clothes online, they face problems with the fitting of clothes as each shop has its own measurements.
Our website will thus encourage sustainable clothing by facilitating the fixing, maintenance and swapping of consumers’ fashion items.
