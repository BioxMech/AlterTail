AlterTail is a one-stop fashion maintenance portal for residents in Singapore. Launched in 2020, it is a platform tailored for the region. We aim to connect users with fashion service providers such as alteration service, tailors and dry-cleaners, providing users with an easy, secure and fast online shopping experience through strong payment and fulfilment support.

Our platform offers a wide spectrum of businesses as we want to offer our customers options from both the higher-end and also on the affordable end. This encapsulates the more exquisite retail businesses and the more neighbourhood-friendly home-based businesses, bringing the best of both worlds to our customers.

Our company stemmed from the underlying belief that the rise of fast fashion which provides easy access to the purchase of clothes online leads to customers facing an increase need of such fashion services. We believe fashion services should be accessible, easy and enjoyable. This is the vision AlterTail aspires to deliver on the platform, every single day.
